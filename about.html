Ben worked for close to a decade in the non-profit sector as a rabbi, educator and community organizer. He served communities in Boston, Denver and New York and worked on social justice issues in Chicago. He was a recipient of a Rabbis Without Borders fellowship in 2010, was a member of the Board of Directors of the International Rabbinic Fellowship and a member of the New York Board of Rabbis. He is the author of two books and numerous articles that are published in The Huffington Post, Time Magazine, The Boston Globe, The Denver Post, MyJewishLearning.com and other platforms. He is currently a student at the Flatiron School studying full stack web development. Ben is married and lives with his wife and children in the Bronx.
